SMART PARKING HUB — KNOWLEDGE BASE
====================================

GENERAL INFORMATION
-------------------
Parking Name: Smart Parking Hub
Location: Bhubaneswar, Odisha
Working Hours: 24/7 — open every day, all day
Total Capacity: 100 slots across all vehicle types
Contact: smartparking@hub.in


PRICING
-------
Pricing is based on vehicle type:

- Car:             ₹50 per hour
- Motorcycle:      ₹30 per hour
- SUV:             ₹70 per hour
- Electric Vehicle: ₹50 per hour (EV charging included at no extra cost)
- Truck:           ₹100 per hour

Daily maximum cap: ₹500 for cars, ₹300 for motorcycles, ₹700 for SUVs.
Monthly passes are available on request.


PARKING SLOTS
-------------
Slots are organized by vehicle type and located across different zones:

Car Slots (Zone A and Zone B and Zone C):
  - A1, A2, A3, B1, B2, B3, C1, C2
  - Total: 8 car slots
  - Suitable for standard sedans and hatchbacks

Motorcycle Slots (Zone M):
  - M1, M2, M3, M4, M5
  - Total: 5 motorcycle slots
  - Covered parking available

SUV Slots (Zone S):
  - S1, S2, S3, S4
  - Total: 4 SUV slots
  - Extra-wide bays for large vehicles

Electric Vehicle Slots (Zone EV):
  - EV1, EV2, EV3
  - Total: 3 EV slots
  - All EV slots are equipped with fast charging stations (Level 2 AC charging)
  - Charging is included in the ₹50/hour rate

Truck Slots (Zone T):
  - T1, T2
  - Total: 2 truck slots
  - Height clearance: up to 4 metres


BOOKING PROCESS
---------------
To reserve a parking slot:
1. Choose your vehicle type from the sidebar
2. Select your preferred parking slot
3. Set your parking duration (1 to 24 hours)
4. Type "book parking" in the chat or click "Start Booking Now"
5. Provide your name, surname, car number, and date & time
6. Your reservation request is sent to the administrator for approval
7. Once approved, you receive a unique Reservation ID (e.g. RES-8B22207F)
8. Use your Reservation ID to check your status at any time

To check reservation status:
- Go to the "Reservation Status" tab
- Enter your Reservation ID
- Or type: check reservation RES-XXXXXXXX in the chat

VEHICLE TYPES ACCEPTED
----------------------
The following vehicle types are accepted:
1. Car (sedans, hatchbacks, coupes)
2. Motorcycle (bikes, scooters)
3. SUV (sports utility vehicles, MPVs)
4. Electric Vehicle (any EV or hybrid — gets charging access)
5. Truck (commercial vehicles, vans)

Each vehicle type has dedicated slots to avoid congestion.


AVAILABILITY
------------
- Car slots (A1–C2): typically available, 8 slots total
- Motorcycle slots (M1–M5): always high availability, 5 slots
- SUV slots (S1–S4): moderate demand, book in advance for weekends
- EV slots (EV1–EV3): limited — only 3 slots, book early
- Truck slots (T1–T2): very limited — only 2 slots, advance booking recommended


CANCELLATION POLICY
-------------------
- Cancellations made more than 2 hours before reservation time: full refund
- Cancellations within 2 hours: 50% charge applies
- No-shows: full charge applies


SECURITY
--------
- All reservations require administrator approval before confirmation
- A unique Reservation ID is issued for every confirmed booking
- Token-authenticated API processes all reservation data
- Personal data is stored securely and not shared with third parties
